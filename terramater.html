Why We Care

As inhabitants of Earth, we have the reponsibility of maintaining the planet that allows us to live. We are using the resources of this planet with disregard of the future generations and the health of Terra Mater.
We started our website with the goal of spreading awareness of the environmental issues we are facing today in the hopes of expressing the gravity of our situation. As members of the recent generation, we are passionate about creating a better
tomorrow for our future families.
